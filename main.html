About The University :

Gujarat University is a well reputed State government University and was established in 1990, having a campus spread over more than 400 acres of land with world class infrastructure and more than 70,000 students on campus. The University offers Diplomas, Under Graduate, Post–Graduate and under the Faculties of Engineering and Technology, Pharmacy, Management, Computer Applications. The University is also offering unique specialisation courses in disciplines of Computer Science and Engineering in collaboration with IBM. The University also has affiliations with various foreign universities and also runs student exchange programs.
