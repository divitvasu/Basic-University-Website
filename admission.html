Admission Detail:



ADMISSION TO ENGINEERING PROGRAMS

Admission to the First Year of the Bachelor of Technology shall be given as under, namely:

Gujarat Seats (50%)

For the purpose of admission on Gujarat Seats, a candidate has to apply at ACPC and fulfill the criteria as per ACPC norms.

All India Seats (35%)

A candidate seeking admission on All India Seats shall apply on-line, for the registration of his candidature, on the website, within the time limit specified. Thirty five percent seats shall be reserved by university, for those candidates who have passed the Qualifying Examination from the schools located in India and have appeared in JEE (Main) 2016.

NRI Seats (15%)

For the purpose of admission on NRI/NRI Sponsored Seats, a candidate has to apply separately through website.



ELIGIBILITY FOR ADMISSION

For the purpose of admission on All India Seats, a candidate should apply and fulfill criteria as mentioned below:

1. A candidate appeared in Paper 1 of JEE (Main) conducted in 2016

2. A candidate should have passed the Qualifying Examination with minimum 45% marks (40% in case of SC / ST) in aggregate in theory and practical of Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.
